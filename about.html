About

Hello! I am a writer, skier, and adventurer based in Denver, Co. I am currently a student in The New School's (NYC) Riggio Writing & Democracy program. I enjoy writing about culture, the human condition, and the great outdoors. You can find my selected writings here.

I was born and raised in Aspen, Co. Before attending The New School and pursuing a career in writing, I was a member of the University of Colorado's NCAA Div 1 Ski Team, where I was the Team Captain and letter winner.

If you are interested in working together, you can find me at Iambobbymoyer [at] Gmail [dot] com, or follow me on @sonic.bob and/or Twitter @Bobby_moyer13.
